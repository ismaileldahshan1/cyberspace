Search and rescue mission in a disaster area. The drone flies over the area and locates two survivors. Survivor 1 is at coordinates (2.0, 5.0) and Survivor 2 is at coordinates (8.0, 3.0). The drone communicates Survivor 1 coordinates (2.0, 5.0) to the Robot Dog and Survivor 2 coordinates (8.0, 3.0) to the TurtleBot3. The Robot Dog navigates to (2.0, 5.0) and delivers a first-aid kit to Survivor 1. The TurtleBot3 navigates to (8.0, 3.0) and delivers supplies to Survivor 2. All robots return to base after completing their tasks.
