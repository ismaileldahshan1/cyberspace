A two-robot building inspection mission. The drone takes off and performs an aerial scan of the perimeter of a building, detecting any open doors or windows and recording their coordinates. For each open entry point it finds, the drone communicates the coordinates to the TurtleBot3 on the ground. The TurtleBot3 then navigates to each entry point one at a time, performs a close-up ground-level inspection using its camera, and reports the findings back through the APM. Once all entry points are inspected, both robots return to base.
